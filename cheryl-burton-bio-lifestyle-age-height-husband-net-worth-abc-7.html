CAREER OF CHERYL BURTON

Burton began his career appearing on the American children’s television series Romper Room. After that, she appeared as a candidate on Star Search. After that, she hosted an hour-long cable TV show called Simply Elegant. She began her journalism career as a co-anchor on a nationally broadcast weekly series “MBR: The Minority Business Report” in 1989.

The following year Cheryl moved to Peoria, Illinois where she worked as a reporter at WMBD-TV. She then became an anchor at KWCH-DT in Wichita, Kansas in 1990. Two years later, she joined WLS-TV in 1992 as a co-anchor and weekend reporter.
