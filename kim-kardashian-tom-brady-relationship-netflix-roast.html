Kim Kardashian And Tom Bradys Relationship Is More Confusing Than Ever After His Netflix Roast

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1141 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Highlights

  • Kim Kardashian playfully dodged dating rumors with Tom Brady at his Netflix roast.
  • Tom Brady defended Kardashian after she got booed with a light-hearted dig.
  • Rumors of Kim Kardashian and Tom Brady dating surfaced in May 2023, but both denied any romantic involvement.

Kim Kardashian is back in the spotlight after attending Netflix's first-ever live roast for Tom Brady on Sunday, May 5, The Greatest Roast of All Time: Tom Brady. The live roast lasted three hours, was unedited, and was just the start of Netflix's journey into live programming.

The former NFL quarterback took hilarious punches from some of his closest friends, athletes, and celebrities, including Kardashian. Most of the jokes revolved around his divorce from supermodel Gisele Bündchen and his love life, including rumors about him dating Kardashian. Not one to shy away from fame or romance-fueled rumors, Kardashian looked happy on stage as she roasted her former rumored love interest. Knowing Kardashian's love life is constantly in the press, things could be a little tricky between her and Brady after her appearance wasn't well-received by audiences. Below, we'll take a closer look at what Kim Kardashian revealed about her relationship with Tom Brady on The Greatest Roast of All Time: Tom Brady, how their relationship began, and where they stand today.

Kim Kardashian Won't Reveal If She Dated Tom Brady At His Netflix Roast

Although Kim Kardashian and Tom Brady have known each other for a few years, their hypothetical relationship became complicated at the live Netflix roast.

"I wasn’t going to come tonight, but since I'm not here as Tom's date, there's still a good chance I might," Kardashian quipped. She said, "Speaking of Tom and I dating, I know there were some rumors that we were, and I'd never say if we did or not. I'd just release the tape."

The tape, of course, is in reference to her infamous sex tape with R&B singer Ray J.

Kardashian continued making digs at Brady's expense during the roast, while also bringing up some of the more juicy topics that made her famous.

Unfortunately, it looks like fans will never know if Kardashian and Brady dated unless one of them came clean about their time together, but her roast suggested that it would never work out because of Brady's resemblance to her stepdad, Caitlyn Jenner. "I do know it would never work out. An ex-athlete, high cheekbones, silky hair, you remind me too much of my stepdad now," she joked.

Where Do Kim Kardashian And Tom Brady Stand Today?

After three hours of getting roasted by his close friends, Tom Brady took the comments in jest. However, seeing as Kim Kardashian got booed at a roast that wasn't for her, Brady came to her defense lightheartedly, but not without some shade.

Brady later took the mic and made a dig at Kardashian to make it an equal playing field.

"I know Kim [Kardashian] was terrified to be here tonight. Not because of this, but because her kids are at home with their dad," the famous quarterback joked.

Seeing as Kardashian's ex-husband Kanye West has been in hot water as of late for his toxic workplace lawsuits, political points of view, and relationship with his wife Bianca Censori, the dig could complicate things for Kardashian and Brady if they ever wound up dating in the future. Past insults towards one another in the press don't typically bode well for future couples.

Kim Kardashian Was Booed At Tom Brady's Roast

Wearing a tight black dress, diamonds, and holding a glass of champagne, Kim Kardashian looked flawless as she was escorted to the podium by actor and comedian Jeff Ross. However, the cheers slowly turned into booing once she thanked the crowd. Her smile turned crooked as host Kevin Hart jumped in to save Kardashian and lighten things up before she carried on with her roast, trying to take the criticism with grace.

At the same time, Brady smiled and laughed at Kardashian's digs, which centered around their rumored dating life, Caitlyn Jenner, and her former adoration for NFL players. Seeing as only the best of the best were invited to Brady's roast, fans quickly wondered if the two did date and when the rumors started.

When Did The Dating Rumors Start Between Kim Kardashian And Tom Brady?

Back in October 2022, Cosmopolitan shared that Tom Brady and Gisele Bündchen divorced. As two highly publicized public figures, fans of the former couple wanted to know who they were going to date next. In May 2023, it was shared by People that Kim Kardashian was the next gorgeous celebrity that Tom Brady was potentially courting.

Shortly after the rumors flooded the internet, reps for the pair quickly denied their dating.
