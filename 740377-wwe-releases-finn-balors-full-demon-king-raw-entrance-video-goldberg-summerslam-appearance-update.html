Goldberg-SummerSlam Appearance Update

Yesterday PWInsider.com reported that Bill Goldberg would be in New York this coming SummerSlam weekend likely doing promotional work for WWE 2K17.

Since it was announced that Goldberg would be in town for SummerSlam weekend the rumors began to fly online about a potential SummerSlam appearance. F4WOnline.com is playing down the reports and noting that Goldberg’s Friday WWE 2K Sports media appearance was already expected. Goldberg is coming to NY just for 2K games and not for the WWE. While it is possible that there could be some talks with WWE while he is in town that is not a guarantee.
