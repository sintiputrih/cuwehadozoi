Rue21 Dress Code: What You Need to Know Before You Shop

Rue21 is an American fashion retailer that specializes in affordable and trendy clothing for young adults. With over 700 stores across the United States, Rue21 has become a popular destination for those looking to update their wardrobe. However, with any workplace, there are certain dress codes that must be followed. In this article, we’ll dive into what you need to know about Rue21’s dress code policy, including frequently asked questions and guidelines on what to wear.

What is Rue21’s Dress Code Policy?

Rue21’s dress code policy is fairly relaxed, as they aim to encourage self-expression and creativity in their employees. However, the company still has guidelines in place to ensure that employees are presenting themselves in a professional and appropriate manner while representing the brand. The following are some key points to keep in mind:

– Employees are encouraged to wear clothing that represents current fashion trends while being modest and appropriate for the workplace
– Clothing with offensive language, graphics, or logos are not allowed
– Hats, hoods, and sunglasses are prohibited indoors
– Flip flops and open-toed shoes are allowed as long as they are clean and in good condition
– Backless or see-through clothing is not allowed
– Clothing should be clean and free of holes or tears
– Hair should be clean and well-groomed
– Visible tattoos and piercings are allowed, but facial piercings should be small and discreet.

What Should You Wear to an Interview with Rue21?

When interviewing with Rue21, it’s important to present yourself in a professional manner. While the company’s dress code policy is fairly relaxed, you’ll want to make sure that you’re dressing to impress. Here are some guidelines to keep in mind:

– For women, consider wearing a knee-length skirt or dress pants paired with a blouse or sweater
– For men, a nice pair of dress pants and a button-down shirt or sweater is appropriate
– Avoid wearing anything too casual, such as jeans or athletic wear
– Shoes should be clean and in good condition
– Keep accessories to a minimum and avoid anything too flashy.
