Emma Watson Kisses New Boyfriend After London Lunch Date

 Posted on May 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  287 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Emma Watson was photographed in London this week kissing a new man.
  • The actress had just finished a lunch date when the pair kissed outside of Gail's Bakery.
  • It had previously been rumored that Watson was dating Harry Potter co-star, Tom Felton.

It looks like there's a new man in Emma Watson's life.

The actress, who will soon be seen in Greta Gerwig's Little Women, had a very public lunch date with a mystery man this week. Having dined at Gail's Bakery in London, the actress was snapped doing some serious multi-tasking. Watson was on the phone when her new beau went in for a kiss.

Obviously, the pair looked pretty smitten with one another, although the identity of Watson's date is still unknown. Watson kept her outfit casual in a striped sweater, and wooly hat.

BACKGRID

Watson's kissing session puts rumors of a Draco Malfoy hookup to rest. Fans previously believed that Watson might be dating her Harry Potter co-star, Tom Felton, after he posted an Instagram snap of the actress in her pajamas over the summer. However, it would now seem that Watson and Felton are just friends.

Watson is notoriously private when it comes to her relationships. In March 2017, she told Vanity Fair, "I want to be consistent: I can’t talk about my boyfriend in an interview and then expect people not to take paparazzi pictures of me walking around outside my home. You can’t have it both ways."
