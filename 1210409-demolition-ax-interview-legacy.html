Demolitions Ax On Their Tag Team Legacy, Why They Still Resonate With Wrestling Fans

 Posted on October 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  206 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Demolition’s Ax (Bill Eadie) spoke with WrestleZone Managing Editor Bill Pritchard ahead of the duo’s virtual signing with ’80s Wrestling Con on Monday, November 16.

The two discussed:

  • Demolition’s longevity as a team
  • Teams paying homage to them
  • How he would have ended their run after he got sick
  • Road Warrior comparisons
  • How he wants fans to remember their work

and more — listen now!
