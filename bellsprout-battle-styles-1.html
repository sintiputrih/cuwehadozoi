Bellsprout (Japanese: マダツボミ Madatsubomi) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Battle Styles expansion.

Card text

Venoshock
ベノムショック		10+
If your opponent's Active Pokémon is Poisoned, this attack does 40 more damage.

Trivia

Origin

Venoshock is a move in the Pokémon games that Bellsprout can learn via TM. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
