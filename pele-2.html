Pelé Edson popularly known as Arantes do Nascimento is a Brazilian footballer. As of 2023, Pele’s net worth is $100 million. Considered one of the best players of all time and dubbed “the greatest” by FIFA, he was one of the twentieth century’s most successful and popular sports celebrities.

In the year 1999, he was selected International Olympic Committee’s Athlete of the Century and was featured in Time magazine’s list of the century’s 100 most influential individuals. Pelé was named World Player of the Century by the International Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS) in 2000.
