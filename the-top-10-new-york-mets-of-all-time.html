The Top 10 New York Mets of All-Time

 Posted on February 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  675 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The New York Mets, established in 1962, have had numerous talented players represent the team over the years.

From their early years to the present, the Mets have seen some exceptional talent grace the field.

Here, we take a look at the top 10 New York Mets of all time, celebrating their contributions to the team's legacy and their impact on the sport of baseball.

1. Tom Seaver

Position: Pitcher
Years with Mets: 1967-1977, 1983
Accolades: 3x Cy Young Award, 12x All-Star, 1969 World Series Champion

Tom Seaver, also known as "The Franchise", is widely regarded as the greatest Met of all time.

His career with the Mets was filled with numerous accolades, including three Cy Young Awards.

Seaver's remarkable pitching abilities and leadership played a pivotal role in the Mets' 1969 World Series Championship.

2. Dwight Gooden

Position: Pitcher
Years with Mets: 1984-1994
Accolades: 1984 NL Rookie of the Year, 3x All-Star, 1985 NL Cy Young Award

Dwight Gooden burst onto the scene in 1984, winning the NL Rookie of the Year award and displaying electrifying pitching prowess.

His early success continued as he won the Cy Young Award in 1985, solidifying his place as one of the greatest pitchers in Mets history.

3. Darryl Strawberry

Position: Outfielder
Years with Mets: 1983-1990
Accolades: 1983 NL Rookie of the Year, 8x All-Star

Darryl Strawberry was a dominant force in the Mets' lineup throughout the 1980s. His combination of power and speed made him a formidable offensive threat. His impact was instrumental in the Mets' success during that era, including their 1986 World Series win.

4. David Wright

Position: Third Baseman
Years with Mets: 2004-2018
Accolades: 7x All-Star, 2x Gold Glove Award

David Wright, known as "Captain America" to Mets fans, was a cornerstone of the team for almost a decade and a half. His exceptional defensive skills and consistent offensive production solidified his place as one of the greatest Mets players of all time.

5. Jerry Koosman

Position: Pitcher
Years with Mets: 1967-1978
Accolades: 2x All-Star, 1969 World Series Champion

Jerry Koosman was an integral part of the Mets' pitching rotation during the late 1960s and 1970s. His contributions, especially during the Mets' 1969 World Series-winning season.

6. Keith Hernandez

Position: First Baseman
Years with Mets: 1983-1989
Accolades: 5x All-Star, 11x Gold Glove Award

Keith Hernandez's arrival in 1983 brought a new level of leadership and defensive prowess to the Mets. His exceptional fielding skills at first base, combined with his consistent hitting, played a crucial role in the Mets' success during the mid-1980s.

7. Mike Piazza

Position: Catcher
Years with Mets: 1998-2005
Accolades: 12x All-Star, 2000 NL Championship Series MVP

Arguably one of the greatest hitting catchers in baseball history, Mike Piazza left an indelible mark during his time with the Mets. His offensive prowess and impact on the team's performance earned him a place among the top players in Mets history.

8. Jose Reyes

Position: Shortstop
Years with Mets: 2003-2011, 2016-2018
Accolades: 4x All-Star, 2006 Silver Slugger Award

Jose Reyes, known for his electrifying speed and dynamic play, was a fan favorite during his time with the Mets. His ability to change the game with his speed and overall offensive skills made him a standout player.

9. Cleon Jones

Position: Outfielder
Years with Mets: 1963-1975
Accolades: 2x All-Star, 1969 World Series Champion

Cleon Jones, a key member of the 1969 World Series-winning team, provided consistent hitting and defensive stability in the outfield throughout his tenure with the Mets. His contributions to the team's success in 1969 solidify his place among the all-time greats.

10. Ed Kranepool

Position: First Baseman/Outfielder
Years with Mets: 1962-1979
Accolades: 1969 World Series Champion

As one of the original Mets, Ed Kranepool holds a special place in the team's history. His longevity with the franchise and his role in the Mets' 1969 World Series triumph make him a beloved figure among Mets fans.

 

The New York Mets have been home to numerous legendary players who have left an indelible mark on the franchise and the sport of baseball.

These top 10 players have not only achieved personal success but have also contributed to some of the most memorable moments in Mets history, solidifying their places as all-time greats.

Their impact on the team's success and the hearts of Mets fans will be cherished for generations to come.
