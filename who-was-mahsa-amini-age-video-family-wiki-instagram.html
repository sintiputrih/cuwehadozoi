Who was Mahsa Amini? Age, Video, Family, Wiki, Instagram

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  168 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Mahsa Amini was an Iranian woman who was arrested by the Islamic Republic of Iran’s Guidance Patrol for wearing a hijab that did not meet the government’s mandatory Hijab standards.

According to the police report, she suffered from heart failure and died after two days in a coma.

However, additional evidence indicates that her head was repeatedly smashed into a police car with a stick.

Some believe her coma, brain death, and death were caused by these violent acts, which became a symbol of violence against women under the Islamic Republic.

Mahsa Amini Age

How old was Mahsa Amini when she died? Mahsa Amini was born on 22 July 2000 in Saqqez, Iran. She was 22.

Mahsa Amini Family

Mahsa Amini was an Iranian Kurdish woman, originally from Saqqez in Kurdistan province. She has a brother named Kiaresh Amini who was with her at the time of her arrest.
