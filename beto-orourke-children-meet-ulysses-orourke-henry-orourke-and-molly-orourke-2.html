Beto O’Rourke is an American politician who represented Texas’s 16th congressional district in the US House of Representatives from 2013 to 2019. Beto O’Rourke sought the 2020 Democratic Party nomination for the Presidency of the United States and later abandoned it.

Beto O’Rourke is a Catholic and he is a Bachelor of Arts degree holder in English Literature from Columbia University. Beto is in line to compete for the 2021 Texas governor as he has declared his interest.
