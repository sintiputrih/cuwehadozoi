Dino Conner’s H-Town Band, Career & Profession

Delando Omingo Conner was very passionate about singing since his school days. During his school days, he formed a band with his brother Solomon Conner and friend Darryl Jackson. They named their band ‘The Gents’. They also recorded their album ‘It’s No Dream’ but that was not a major hit. They did not lose hope and kept practicing their vocal tone. Afterward, ‘Luke Records’ signed with the group and they renamed their group ‘H-Town’.

H-Town Musical Band and Songs

Conner was the lead vocalist of the band. According to the reports, the group recorded their first album in 1993 titled ‘Fever for Da Flavor’ and was much liked by the people. Then, in 1994, they launched the ‘Beggin’ After Dark’ album. Their third album ‘Ladies Edition, Woman’s World’ was also a major hit.

The group launched their 4th album after the death of Dino and named the album ‘Imitation of Life’. Apart from the album, H-Town also launched several singles as well such as Keepin’ My Composure, Part-Time Lover, A Thin Line Between Love & Hate, Baby I Wanna, and many more.
