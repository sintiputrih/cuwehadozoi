 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1155 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Apink is a South Korean girl group formed by Play M Entertainment and Plan A Entertainment which is consists of 6 members named, Park Cho-rong, Yoon Bo-mi, Jung Eun-ji, Son Na-eun, Kim Nam-joo and Oh Ha-young. In April 2013, Hong left the group to focus on her studies. On April 19, 2011, under ‘Play M Entertainment’ APink band debuted. Even, the group has won awards at shows such as the Golden Disc Awards, the Seoul Music Awards and the Mnet Asian Music Awards. Apink has released eight Korean mini-albums, three Korean studio albums and three Japanese studio albums as well. On Jan 5, 2012, their first music program win was on ‘M Countdown’ “My My” from their second EP Snow Pink.
