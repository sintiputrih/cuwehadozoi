Teen Mom: Jace Evans Disappears For A Second Time

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Teen Mom fans know that Jace Evans lived with his grandmother, Barbara for most of his life. However, he apparently became a bit much for her and she finally handed over custody to her daughter, Jenelle Eason.

However, it seems he’s not happy because the cops had to go and search for him twice in a month.

Teen Mom: Jace Evans Ran Away Earlier This Month

The first news that Jenelle’s son had gone missing came via the Brunswick County Sheriff’s Office, NC. On Facebook, the post updated that he had been found.

However, before that, the post noted that the teenager had last been seen at  “Roger Bacon Academy” in Leland.”

People were asked to keep an eye out for him and call in if they knew where he was.

Teen Mom alum, Jenelle made a statement and asked for privacy for the family. At the time, she just wanted to focus on Jace.

She also claimed that he just ran away because of the way she disciplined him. The MTV alum said that they decided to relieve him of his “phone” after he got into trouble “at school.”

Actually, a  lot of people disbelieved her and on social media, it was noted that David and Jenelle were engaged in a fight online.

Teen Mom: Jace Evans Goes Missing Again

On August 29, TMZ reported that Jace had once again gone missing. Fortunately, he was found after his mom called the cop for assistance.

When the outlet shared its story on Instagram, its caption said, “…Jenelle Evans is dealing with every parent’s nightmare — her son Jace has now gone missing twice in the last two weeks.”

Meanwhile, more details emerged in the article.

The former Teen Mom boy had apparently been wandering around The Land. David and Jenelle have quite a big piece of ground.

The outlet cited Jenelle’s manager as saying that “Jace was located at a gas station about 10 minutes from Jenelle’s home.”

Worried about him, his mom had called the police. At the moment, it’s not clear about any other details.

MTV Fans Comment

Teen Mom fans took to the Instagram comments section. One of them opined, “He obviously doesn’t want to live there. Why take him away from Barbara? I’m sure he wants to be with her.”

Meanwhile, another fan wrote, “[Jenelle’s] Getting back what she gave, terrible cycle. SMH.”

More followed: “This is not normal teen behavior. He clearly doesn’t wanna be there.”
