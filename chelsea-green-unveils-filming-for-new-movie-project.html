Chelsea Green Unveils Filming For New Movie Project

 Posted on January 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  415 words  |  Larita Shotwell

WWE Superstars have a very busy schedule, and that goes double for Chelsea Green this week. She has a lot going on, but she is on her way to doing great things.

Chelsea Green, who is one-half of the WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions alongside Piper Niven, will be present on the November 13th episode of WWE Monday Night Raw as Niven faces Tegan Nox in a singles match. In addition to her wrestling commitments, Chelsea Green has disclosed on Twitter that she will be participating in a movie project in Canada, her home country.

Despite her packed schedule, Chelsea Green intends to spend quality time with her husband, Matt Cardona, known for his monikers as the “Indy God” and “Michael Jordan of action figure collecting.” She did not reveal the name of her movie project, but the WWE Women’s Tag Team Champion is very happy with how things have worked out for her.

Chelsea Green logged onto X, and she updated fans on her upcoming schedule. She is going to be on RAW tonight, and then she’s off to shoot a movie. 2023 has certainly been good to her.

This week I get to be on Monday Night Raw, shoot a movie in Canada & still make it home to kiss my husband. 2023 has blessed me

Regarding the movie project, Chelsea Green did not provide further details at this time. However, she did discuss her previous experience during a 2021 interview with Fightful. In that conversation, she revealed that she had received an offer for a movie role set to be filmed in Canada but that production had been halted due to the global pandemic.

Only time will tell what is next for Chelsea Green in WWE, and the rest of the world. Things seem to be lining up very well for her at this point. Keep checking back with us here at Ringside News for more.
