A Short Bio on Austin Ekeler

The professional American football player Austin Ekeler was born to his mother, Suzanne Ekeler, on May 17, 1995, in Lincoln, Nebraska, United States. His mother divorced his dad when he was a kid, so he spent his childhood with his mother and brother.

His younger brother, Wyett, is also a football player and plays college football at the University of Wyoming.

Austin attended Eaton High School in Colorado and played football for The Fightin’ Reds. After graduation, he enrolled in Western Colorado University in Gunnison and played college football for the Mountaineers.

Unfortunately, Austin remained undrafted in the 2017 NFL draft after his college. The L.A. Chargers, however, saw potential in him and signed a three-year contract worth $1.66 million.

In just three years, Ekeler’s performance established him as one of the best running backs in the league. In 2020, he signed a four-year contract worth nearly $25 million with the Chargers.
