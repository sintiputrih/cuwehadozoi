    Wildcat Facts

  • His best childhood memory is getting a 4-wheeler as a gift from his parents in kindergarten.
  • His worst childhood memory is the 1st day of school in the 4th grade. He had just moved there, and was shy and emotional, and even cried.
  • Wildcat used to play basketball and football as a teenager and young adult. He watched professional football but only college basketball because “NBA is a joke,” according to him.
  • He used to have a plethora of Old Spice deodorants in 2013 because he liked to switch up fragrances.
  • Wildcat did a face reveal on January 10, 2013, in a Q&A Vlog to celebrate reaching 10k subscribers.
  • Someone broke into his 2014 GMC Sierra 1500 truck around January 2014 and stole all of his YouTube stuff, including a computer, monitor, and at least 2 Xboxes. The police never found the perpetrator and he was forced to start from scratch.
  • He created a secondary YouTube channel titled WILDCATLetsPlays in 2015. In September 2019, a hacker group named Chuckling Squad took over control of it.
  • Wildcat is a member of the group of gamers and social media stars, often called ‘The Vanoss Crew’. It consists of VanossGaming, Lui Calibre, H2O Delirious, Mini Ladd, BasicallyIDoWrk, Moo, Smii7y, BigJigglyPanda, and fourzer0seven.
  • He has 3 dogs – Chief, Archie, and Kino. Chief was his parents’ dog and was born in 2008.
  • Wildcat is best known for his exploding rage, insulting and dark humor, and wheezing laugh.
  • He had a running gag on his channel while he played a sandbox video game Garry’s Mod. Instead of using his regular pig character, he used a life-sized Doritos bag, and used to crinkle a Doritos bag every time he moved in-game.
  • There is a superhero named Wildcat in the DC Universe, whose real name is Ted Grant.
  • Wildcat’s primary YouTube channel reached a milestone of 5 million subscribers in April 2017.
  • He is a big fan of luxury and sports cars, particularly Corvette C7, C8, ZR1, and Z06. He also used to ride ATVs in the past.
  • Wildcat has a collection of Nike Air and Nike Air Jordan shoes and likes to play golf in his free time.
  • He once admitted to having “a female ass” which is prominent when he’s wearing jeans.
  • If he weren’t a YouTuber, he jokingly revealed he would have most likely been a cam model. On a serious note, Wildcat stated he would possibly be a video game developer or programmer.
  • His dad, whom he featured in a few videos, is even taller than Wildcat and stands at a height of 6 ft 7 in or 200.5 cm.
