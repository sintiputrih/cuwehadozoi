UPDATE: Janet Jackson Rock Witchu Tour Announces More Dates

 Posted on October 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  326 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Janet Jackson has added more dates to her Rock Witchu Tour which begins in Vancouver and for now is ending in Texas. This is her first tour in seven years.

Janet has left an indelible mark on pop music, selling more than 100 million albums and, having achieved the number one spot on Billboard Magazine's Top 200 Album Chart 6 times, is among just 3 female artists that have achieved such status. She is the only performer male or female to be nominated for Grammy Awards in each of the Pop, Rock, Dance, Rap and R&B categories, and the first female recording artist of color to be nominated for a Producer of the Year Grammy. With literally hundreds of awards and nominations from everyone from the American Music Awards, where she holds the title for most nominations from one album, to MTV, where she was awarded the first ICON award, to the Academy Awards, Golden Globes, Radio Music Awards, World Music Awards, Billboard, Rolling Stone, NAACP, etc., the recognitions of her musical contributions are astounding.
