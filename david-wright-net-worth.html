David Wright | Charity

As one of the highest-earning athletes, David Wright loves to share his earnings with needy people.

In 2005, he established a charity foundation by his name, David Wright Foundation, to increase awareness about multiple sclerosis and to raise money for various sclerosis organizations and projects.

By organizing various programs and annual events, the foundation has been helping to enhance the quality of life for children in the Tri-State and Norfolk areas.

In 2009, Wright and Yankees shortstop Derek Jeter represented their foundations in a competition sponsored by Delta Air Lines.

Wright foundation received a fund of $50,000 in the competition.

Moreover, Wright is the host of an eight-city fantasy football network, Big League Impact of New York City.

The organization was created and endorsed by longtime St. Louis Cardinals pitcher Adam Wainwright.

In 2015, they raised more than $1 million in total for various charitable organizations.
