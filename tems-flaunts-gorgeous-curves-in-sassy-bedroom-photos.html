Nigeria’s Afrobeats sensational singer/songwriter, Tems has taken  took to her Instagram page to flaunt her gorgeous curves in a stunning flowing white dress.

Naija News reports that the singer shared new photos of herself in a luxury bedroom flaunting her curves and flawless back in sexy white dress.

The pretty singer who looked absolutely stunning  posted the photos on her Instagram page shortly after she was listed as winners of the 2022 BET Hip Hop Award.

According to the songstress, she is waiting to unleash her goodness to her yarning fans.

Sharing the breathtaking photos, she wrote; ‘Waiting to unleash.’

Naija News reports that the BET Hip Hop Awards was held on 4th October 2022.

Tems earned three nominations for her contribution on Future’s ‘Wait For U’ namely- Song of the Year, Best Hip Hop Video, and Best Collaboration Award.

‘Wait For U’ edged out other nominees including ‘Family Ties’ by Baby Keem & Kendrick Lamar, ‘Way 2 Sexy’ by Drake Featuring Future & Young Thug, ‘Hot Shit’ by Cardi B, Kanye West & Lil Durk to win the Best Collaboration Award.
