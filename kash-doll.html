Kash Doll (born March 14, 1992) is an American Rapper, Songwriter, Actress and Model from Detroit, Michigan. She began her career by releasing a remix version of Tinashe’s “2 On” in 2014. Furthermore, the 26-year-old rapper later collaborated with artists like Philthy Rich, Compton AV, Icewear Vezzo, Lil Stl, Ca$h Out, Young Gator etc.

Doll released her first single i.e. the cover of AV’s “Run Me My Money” in 2015. Moreover, she gained good reviews from the public. Later, the rapper came out with her first mixtape “K.R.E.A.M. Kash Rules Everything Around Me” in 2015.

Her real full birth name is Arkeisha Knight. The star’s mixtape enabled her to sign a record deal with BMB Records. In addition, she released “Keisha Vs. Kash Doll” mixtape. It also featured stars like Lyrica Anderson, Zaytoven, Trina, and B.O.B.

Knight’s first extended play titled “Kashin’ Out” came out through RMMR record label. In fact, she also got the chance to perform with Drake during the Summer 26 tour in Detroit. Her most recent mixtape “Brat Mail’” released in March 2018.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

Arkeisha Knight was born on 14th March 1992 in Detroit, Michigan, United States. Therefore, Kash Doll’s age is 26 years old. Her father died when the rapper was very young. Later her mother remarried with her stepfather.

Furthermore, Doll has six siblings (names are not available). She graduated from Detroit City High School. The rapper also faced a lot of teasing at school. However, Knight was also one of the most popular girls in her school due to her talent.

Moreover, Arkeisha also did several odd jobs like babysitting and being a janitor at Ford Field in order to support her big family. She also worked at Best Buy, Better Made Chip factory, and Little Caesar’s Pizza.

Personal Affairs, Husband & Children

The 26-year-old was in a relationship with her boyfriend named Shawn. However, they later developed some problems in their relationship and parted their ways. According to a source, Knight also dated Canadian Rapper “Drake”.
