Marisol Nichols Biography

Marisol Nichols is an American actress. She is famously known for starring Nadia Yassir on the Fox series 24 and Hermione Lodge on the CW drama series Riverdale. In the 1990s, she became a Scientologist, having been introduced to it by her chiropractor.

Marisol Nichols Family

Marisol was born in the Rogers Park neighborhood of Chicago, Illinois, and raised in  Naperville, Illinois. She was raised by her mother Maria Patnick (an American of Mexican ancestry) and her stepfather Randy. Her biological father is of Hungarian-Jewish descent. Marisol has two younger brothers.

Marisol Nichols Husband

Marisol is currently married to Taron Lexton. The couple tied the knot in 2008. Together, they have been blessed with a daughter (Rain India Lexton) who was born in September 2008. In November 2018, Marisol filed for divorce. In 2020, the divorce was final. Marisol was previously married to Andrea Sorrentino. The couple met in Italy while filming the movie My Father’s Shoes and later tied the knot in 1999. They later divorced. Marisol currently resides in Los Angeles, California.
