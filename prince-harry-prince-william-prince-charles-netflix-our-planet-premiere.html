Prince Harry, Prince Charles, and Prince William Attend Netflix Documentary Premiere

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Prince Harry and Prince William will join Prince Charles for a special engagement on April 4.
  • The princes will accompany their father to the London premiere of Netflix's Our Planet.
  • The nature documentary is set to "examine how climate change impacts all living creatures," per Netflix.

Prince Charles, Prince Harry, and Prince William are supporting Sir David Attenborough by attending the London premiere of his new Netflix series on April 4. All three princes will attend the event together, without their wives.

As reported by royal blogger Gert's Royals on Twitter, "New Engagement: April 4th, Prince William & Prince Harry will join Prince Charles at the Global Premiere of @NetflixUK’s Our Planet. The event will be hosted by Sir David Attenborough & take place at @NHM_London."

According to Netflix, Sir David Attenborough's new series will allow viewers to "Experience our planet's natural beauty and examine how climate change impacts all living creatures in this ambitious documentary of spectacular scope." Revealing further details about the incredible new series on YouTube, Netflix's official account wrote, "Share the wonder of the extraordinary place we call home. Utilizing the latest technology Our Planet was filmed entirely in Ultra High Definition in over 50 countries. From exotic jungles to the deepest seas, open your eyes to the connections we all share. Narrated by Sir David Attenborough, Our Planet launches worldwide April 5th."

This week, Charles and his wife Camilla, Duchess of Cornwall, have been undertaking a royal visit to six countries and one overseas territory in the Caribbean, visiting St. Lucia, Barbados, St. Vincent and the Grenadines, St. Kitts and Nevis, Grenada, Cuba, and the Cayman Islands. The prince's official website notes that Charles will be the first member of the royal family to visit Cuba.
