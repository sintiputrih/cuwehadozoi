Helena Mattsson On General Hospital

So recently, Helena Mattsson fills in for her Sofia Mattsson as Sasha on the TV  show General Hospital.

On 17 February 2022-episode of the longest-running American soap opera Sofia was notably absent. And later the fans were made aware that she is passing on the baton to her older sister, Helena, for the time being.

The last few episodes of General Hospital portrayed Sasha’s struggling with the loss of her baby. While in the real world, Sofia who had been playing Sasha became a new mom as she welcomed a baby boy on 26 July 2021.

Sofia had gone on month-long maternity leave in September 2021, resuming work in October 2021. But months of every filming and the current storyline on the show seemed to have taken some kind of toll on her.

Helena, on the other hand, was pretty excited to fill in her sister’s role. “Such a blast jumping in on my sister’s show for the day,” she wrote on social media. Though the way she said ‘for the day’ the change is likely gonna be short-lived.

The news came around a week after Sofia proudly became a United States citizen.

Sofia, known for her works in Becoming Bond (2017), StartUp (2016), and Drive Share (2017), is a star in her own right.

Is Helena Mattsson Dating Anyone?

Helena Mattsson Family

Helena and Sofia Mattsson have two more sisters and they are Mia and Cecilia Mattsson.

Following in her sisters’ footsteps, Mia has also gone on to pursue a career in the same industry. Although hers is in the art and casting department. As per IMDB, Mia has worked as a painter on many TV series/ TVmini series. As a prop maker, she worked on some 24 episodes of the TV series Julens hjältar. Mia was also the assistant production designer in a few episodes of Bibliotekstjuven, a TV mini-series.

Anyway, there Mattsson sisters have always been each other’s keenest supporters.

Meanwhile, Helena also has maintained a very close connection with her parents. She visits them in Stockholm bi-annually for Christmas and summer holidays.

By the look of Helena and her sisters’ social media, we can tell it is from their beautiful mom that they have derived their looks. Especially, Helena is a carbon copy of her mother, with the same eyes and same golden locks.
