Ciaras Wedding Dress: Shop Three Look-Alike Gowns

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  255 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

From a haute couture atelier to your own aisle! Ciara chose a stunning lace wedding dress to marry Russell Wilson in a storybookworthy wedding in Cheshire, England, on Wednesday, July 6. Love her style, brides-to-be? Us Weekly found three more just like it!

PHOTOS: The Best Celeb Wedding Dresses of All Time

The 30-year-old “Body Party” singer’s custom Roberto Cavalli by Peter Dundas gown featured a lace-up bustier-style bodice, a body-conscious skirt with a wider train over it and boho-inspired bell sleeves. While that combination is somewhat hard to find on its own, at least one of those elements can be found in each of these dresses.

Ciara wasn’t the only lady who slayed at her ceremony. The Grammy-nominated artist put each of her bridesmaids — who included Kelly Rowland and La La Anthony — in custom Michael Costello dresses. “Every girl’s individual style is implemented in the design of their dress, as well as their personality,” Costello shared in a statement. “Ciara made sure of that.”
