Rumors are that Leebeth Young passed away in a car accident. What exactly happened?

Young was a church staff at Fellowship Church of Grapevine and the daughter of pastor Ed Young. She conducted several hymns sessions and worked as the director of praise services. She used her position to talk about the importance of fixing the focus on the positive.

LeeBeth always motivated people with her talk like her father. The father-daughter duo shared a strong bond and often worked together and shared stages. Ed even gave his platform to his daughter to share her stories and life journies with people.

Leebeth Young Car Accident Update

There were several rumors that Leebeth Young passed away in a car accident. However, it might not be true, as her parents Ed Young and Lisa Young, have not talked about any car accidents related to their daughter.

The news of Young’s death was announced by her father, Ed Young, on Twitter. He posted a photo of LeeBeth and another photo of a post that announced the news of her death. He said that their precious and cherished daughter passed away.

Leebeth was a talented and devoted woman who left the world early. She struggled with depression and alcoholism and had a rough time figuring out her life.

LeeBeth had a strong bond with her family and often shared pictures with her parents and family on social media. Her family was an extremely important part of her life and often talked about them in public.

Leebeth Young Death Cause: What Exactly Happened?

The cause of Leebeth Young’s death is not solely related to her drinking habits. She used to get seizures after every sustained drinking session.

According to Church Leaders, LeeBeth was battling depression and struggled with loneliness.

Young passed away on January 19, 2021, at the age of 34 years. Her funeral services got held on January 23, 2021. The ceremony included a session of the celebration of life for all the devotion and awareness work she did in her life.

Who Is Leebeth Young?

LeeBeth Young was the oldest daughter of pastor Ed Young and his wife, Lisa Young. She was a director at the Fellowship Church, whereas her parents are the leading pastors at the church.

Ed was a proud father who always posted his daughter’s achievements on social media. According to her father, she was a faithful, intelligent, strong, witty, right, and creative young woman.
