Jen Atkin, aka the hair stylist to the stars, is the mastermind behind this magical shampoo and conditioner. She's worked with everyone from Hailey Bieber to Kendall Jenner to Dua Lipa, so I felt pretty confident that her products would help my hair.

Both products are completely safe for color-treated hair, and they're free of parabens and sulfates.
