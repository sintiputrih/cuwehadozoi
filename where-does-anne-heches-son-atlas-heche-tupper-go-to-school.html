Anne Heche is an American actress born on May 25, 1969, to Nancy and Donald Heche in Aurora, Ohio. She is the youngest of Nancy Heche’s five children.

Heche’s family relocated eleven times while she was growing up, once settling in an Amish neighborhood. Anne started working at a dinner theater in Swainton due to the family’s tight financial situation.

Where does Anne Heche’s son Atlas Heche Tupper go to school?

Heche’s son Atlas whom she had when she was married to Canadian actor James Tupper, attends a private high school in Canada.

According to sources, the 13-year-old’s school is close to Dartmouth in Nova Scotia, Canada.

Heche’s 45-year-old father passed away on March 3, 1983, at the age of 13, from HIV/AIDS, which she thought he acquired from a homosexual lover.

Heche said that from the time she was a baby until the age of 12, he frequently raped her, causing her genital herpes. In a 2001 interview, Heche was questioned “But why would a gay man rape a girl?” and her response was, “I think he was sexually aberrant.”

At the age of 16, Heche was cast in the daytime soap opera As the World Turns when an agent saw her in a school play. Her mother urged that she complete high school before accepting a job opportunity after she traveled to New York City for an audition.

Heche stated, “I served my term with my mum in a shabby one-bedroom apartment and I was done. Heche declined a chance to star in another soap opera, Another World, just before she graduated from high school in 1987 because her mother believed it to be “a sinner’s world.”
