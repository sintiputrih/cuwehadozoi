Who is Colin Kaepernick?

Colin Kaepernick was previously an American quarterback for the National Football League’s San Francisco 49ers from 2011 to 2016. Currently, he is a free agent.

Who are the parents of Colin Kaepernick?

Colin was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin on November 3, 1987. He was just a few weeks old when he was adopted by Rick and Teresa Kaepernick. Additionally, they already had two children of their own but had lost two other babies not long after birth because of heart defects.

His biological mother Heidi Russo was 19 when he was born. He was athletic from an early age. Furthermore, he began playing youth football at age 8. He is of American nationality. Additionally, he belongs to a mixed ethnic background of African-American, Bohemian Czech, and Irish.

Talking about his education, Colin initially attended grade school in Turlock, California. Later, he attended John H. Pitman High School. Furthermore, he received a scholarship at the University of Nevada.

Colin Kaepernick: Career, Salary, and Net Worth

In the University of Nevada, Colin Kaepernick stepped in to play QB in the fifth game of his freshman year. He finished the year with 19 touchdowns.

Later, he set several school records and became the first quarterback in the history of Division I FBS to pass for more than 10,000 yards and rush for more than 4,000 yards. The San Francisco 49ers selected the quarterback in the second round of the 2011 NFL draft.

Colin took over as the team’s No. 1 quarterback in 2012. Furthermore, Jim Harbaugh made him his permanent starting quarterback. He also set a new NFL single-game record for a quarterback. Colin and the 49ers fell to Ray Lewis and the Baltimore Ravens at Super Bowl XLVII in New Orleans.

In the 2013 season, he passed 412 yards and had three touchdowns. Additionally, the 49ers went on to notch a 12-4 record and earn a playoff berth. Furthermore, the 49ers slipped to an 8-8 mark in 2014. Recently, he received Sports Illustrated magazine’s Muhammad Ali Legacy Award.

Colin had a salary of $645,000 in 2014. In addition, he has an estimated net worth of $20 million as of 2023.

Colin Kaepernick: Rumors and Controversy

Colin Kaepernick became involved in a heated controversy after he refused to stand for the national anthem before a preseason game in late August 2016.

Furthermore, he also added that he would continue to sit during the national anthem until seeing a significant change for minorities. The controversy received further attention after U.S. President Donald Trump weighed in by calling for kneeling NFL players to be fired during a September rally in Alabama.

Additionally, Colin filed a grievance against NFL owners for collusion. This attracted number of controversies along its way. Currently, there is no rumor concerning Colin at present.
