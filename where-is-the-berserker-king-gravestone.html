1. How do I start the Side Quest Favor – Fit for a King – and begin the hunt for Berserkers?

To start the Side Quest Favor – Fit for a King – and begin the hunt for Berserkers, you must first reach Chapter 9: Word of Fate. Along the trail of this main quest, you’ll arrive at the King’s Grave in the northeastern reaches of the Lake of Nine.

2. Where is the final Berserker gravestone?

The final Berserker gravestone in Svartalfheim is found in the Jarnsmida Pitmines, but it’s most easily reached by backtracking from the mystic gateway at The Applecore to the mine entrance, where the gravestone can be found in the open area there.

3. Which is the hardest Berserker gravestone?

The King Hrólf’s Gravestone in Midgard is easily the most difficult of the Berserker bosses and is one of the toughest bosses in the entire game. Players need only return to where they originally found the Hilt of Skofnung, which will be right beside the King’s Grave Mystic Gateway.

4. Where is King Berserker in God of War Ragnarok?

Once Kratos has successfully cleared all Berserker Gravestone locations, he will be eligible to take on the Berserker King in Midgard. Follow the guide markers to King’s Grave in the north-eastern section of the map. Return to the very spot Kratos found the hilt, right in the center of the arena.

5. Who is the hardest Berserker in God of War Ragnarok?

King Hrolf Kraki is easily the hardest Berserker in God of War Ragnarok. Be prepared for a challenging fight.

6. Potenital Bug with King’s Grave Berserker Gravestone – God of War Ragnarok

There is no information available about a potential bug with the King’s Grave Berserker Gravestone in God of War Ragnarok. It seems to be working as intended in the game.

7. What is the best armor in God of War Ragnarok?

The Steinbjörn armor set is considered one of the best in the game. It offers high defense and a skill that allows you to restore health by dealing damage. It is worth the time commitment needed to acquire all the pieces.

8. What is the reward for killing Berserker king?

Defeating the Berserker King offers powerful rewards in God of War Ragnarok. One of the rewards is the Hilt of Skofnung Relic, which allows Kratos to summon spectral swords and bolster his offense or push back foes to defend him.

9. What is the easiest Berserker?

Harđrefill is considered the easiest of the Berserker boss battles in God of War Ragnarok. Be prepared for a challenging fight.

10. Where do I go after killing all Berserkers?

After killing all the Berserkers in God of War Ragnarok, players who have completed the other realms’ Berserker Gravestones will be able to challenge King Hrolf, the Berserker King at King’s Grove.

11. What is the best armor to defeat King Hrolf?

To defeat King Hrolf, it is recommended to have the Steinbjörn armor set, fully upgraded to at least level 8. This armor set offers high defense and will help you withstand the tough fight.

12. How do you beat King Berserker in God of War?

To beat King Berserker in God of War, it is recommended to use L1 + triangle moves to stun and deal extra damage. Managing your rage and using it strategically can also help stun the boss and regain health. Be prepared for a challenging battle.

13. What is the soulless warrior in God of War Ragnarok?

The Berserker Cuirass offers the perk “Soulless Warrior” in God of War Ragnarok. This perk turns Kratos into a powerful glass cannon, temporarily increasing his damage output whenever he uses a Relic.

14. What is the best armor for Berserkers?

The recommended armor for Berserkers in God of War Ragnarok is the Necron’s armor. It offers high Strength and Crit Damage, making it a powerful choice for Berserker battles. However, it is an expensive and late-endgame armor set.

15. How do you unlock Niflheim in Berserker gravestone?

To unlock Niflheim in the Berserker gravestone quest, go to Sindri’s House, speak with Ratatoskr, pick up the seeds he offers, and then you can explore the area freely.

16. What is undiscovered in Sanctuary Grove?

In Sanctuary Grove, the Mirkweed flower is undiscovered until you have completed The Path quests in God of War Ragnarok. Once completed, you can find the Mirkweed flower in Sanctuary Grove.

17. Can you beat all Berserkers before Ragnarok?

No, it is not possible to beat all Berserkers before Ragnarok in God of War. You will need access to a location in Helheim, which can only be reached after completing the main story.

18. What is the strongest weapon in Berserk?

The strongest weapon in Berserk is Skull Knight’s Sword of Actuation. It is a powerful weapon that ranks number one among all the weapons in Berserk.

19. Which order should I fight the Berserkers?

The recommended order to fight the Berserkers in God of War Ragnarok is as follows: first, the duo in Svartelfheim Applecore, then the trio in Alfheim Barrens, and finally, the Berserker in Vanaheim Crater Sinkholes.

20. What level should I be to fight Berserker King?

To fight Berserker King in God of War Ragnarok, it is recommended to be at power level 9 and have upgraded your armor and weapons to at least level 8.

21. How do you face Berserker King?

To face the Berserker King in God of War Ragnarok, you need to have cleared all the Berserker Gravestone locations. Follow the guide markers to King’s Grave in the north-eastern section of the map and be prepared for a challenging boss battle.
