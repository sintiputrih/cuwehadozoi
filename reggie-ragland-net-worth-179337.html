About

Linebacker who was drafted in the second round at #41st overall by the Buffalo Bills in the 2016 NFL Draft. He first rose to prominence playing for Nick Saban's Alabama Crimson Tide, where he won the 2015-2016 National Championship as a senior.

Before Fame

He played high school football at Bob Jones High School. He ended his senior season ranked as a 4-star recruit by Rivals, and the #1 middle linebacker in the class of 2012.

Trivia

He was named the SEC Defensive Player of the Year at the conclusion of the 2015 regular season. He won the SEC Championship as a member of the Alabama Crimson Tide in three out of his four years as a student-athlete there.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Huntsville, Alabama.

Associated With

During draft preparations in 2016, he was compared favorably to former NFL linebacker Bart Scott by Bills head coach Rex Ryan.
